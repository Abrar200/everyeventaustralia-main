About Us

Every Event Australia, Australia's First Online Events Marketplace & Venue Directory, was created after our founder Mariah, planned her own wedding in 2020 and realised the huge need for a centralised platform where all event hire and services across different vendors could be easily viewed and booked online - without the need for the back and forth between client and vendor.

"The idea is to simplify the event and wedding planning process while showing transparent prices and no booking fees." - Mariah
