About Us

Hi there and welcome!

I'm Mariah, Founder and Director of Every Event Australia, Australia's First Online Events Marketplace & Venue Directory.

EEA was created after planning my own wedding in 2020 and realising the huge need for a centralised platform where all event hire and services across different vendors could be easily viewed and booked online - without the need for the back and forth between client and vendor. The idea is to simplify the event and wedding planning process while ensuring transparent pricing for our clients.

How it works:

  • Explore vendor listings, view photos of products and services with descriptions, and compare prices and packages
  • Once you've found an item or service you love, simply add it to your cart, enter your event details and book it on the spot!
  • The vendor(s) will then confirm the order within 24-48 hours.

All products and services can be hired or purchased for the exact same price as if you were going directly to the vendor, but this way, you can see all your options upfront, keep track of your orders, communicate with your vendors in one location, and have automatic payments set up.

If you have a question about a product, click the "message vendor" button on the product page to get in touch with the vendor.

The best part? We are completely FREE to use with no booking fees!

Our customer promise:

  • Hire, buy, and book directly from trusted and approved Melbourne & Adelaide Event Vendors.
  • See hundreds of options with pricing upfront.
  • Easy booking system with orders confirmed by the vendor within 24-48hrs.
  • Keep track of your event in one place.
  • Secure online transactions.
  • No booking fees.
  • Easy, stress-free event planning!
